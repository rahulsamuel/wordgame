# **App Name**: Word Scramble Dash

## Core Features:

- Playable Word Grid: Displays an interactive grid of letters where players can find hidden words.
- Dynamic Word & Grid Generation (AI): An AI tool that creates a unique word scratch puzzle, including selecting a word list and strategically embedding words into the letter grid for varied difficulty, based on selected themes.
- Target Word List: Presents a list of words that players need to find within the letter grid.
- 3-Minute Countdown Timer: A clear, visual timer that counts down from 3 minutes for each game round.
- Word Selection Mechanism: Allows players to 'scratch' or select words they find in the grid, highlighting them when correctly identified.
- Score Tracking & Feedback: Awards points for each found word and displays the current score and found words progress.
- Game Over & Results Screen: Shows the player's final score, remaining time (if applicable), and words missed once the 3-minute timer expires.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Energetic Orange (#F0A917), chosen to evoke a sense of playfulness and vibrancy suitable for a child's game.
- Background color: Soft Pale Yellow (#F9F4EA), a heavily desaturated version of the primary hue, providing a light and inviting canvas.
- Accent color: Playful Hot Pink (#F92774), providing high contrast and an additional pop of fun, approximately 30 degrees to the 'left' of the primary color on the color wheel.
- Headline and Body font: 'Poppins', a geometric sans-serif, for a modern, precise, and easily readable appearance that works well for both titles and game elements for young users.
- Use simple, recognizable, and slightly playful icons, such as stars for scores, a clock for the timer, and checkmarks for found words.
- Maintain a clean, uncluttered layout with a large, central word grid. Interactive elements like the word list and timer should be clearly visible and accessible.
- Incorporate subtle animations for feedback, such as a quick glow when a word is correctly 'scratched' or a slight pulse for the timer as it counts down.
